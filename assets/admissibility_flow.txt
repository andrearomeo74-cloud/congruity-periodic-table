ADMISSIBILITY FLOW (STRUCTURAL)

Input:
- State(s)
- Transition(s)

Checks:
1. State exists in canonical grammar
2. Transition is structurally allowed
3. No forbidden jump
4. All prerequisites satisfied

Output:
- ADMISSIBLE
- NOT ADMISSIBLE (forbidden)
- NOT ADMISSIBLE (prerequisite violation)

Notes:
- No optimization
- No prediction
- No domain semantics
